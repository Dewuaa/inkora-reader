[Mission: Occupy the space, secure your part, and survive until the end.] After a disagreement with the author, Lee Hee-ro transmigrated to the world of his favourite shounen manga, Adventure King! But what’s this, characters have to work hard for their survival? If you’re a two-bit villain, you get defeated by the protagonist from the get-go?
